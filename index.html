The event

A favorable shift in atmospheric density is coming. The advantage is knowing early.

Hale is forecasting a shift in solar activity and upper-atmospheric density through Summer 2026. For satellites in low-Earth orbit, lower drag means longer time on orbit — and longer time on orbit changes when you launch, when you replenish, and when you spend.

The value isn't the event, it's the horizon: seeing the regime change before it shows up in decay telemetry, while there is still time to act on it.
